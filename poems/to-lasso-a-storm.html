To Lasso A Storm

My princess is like a young mare
The hair in her wind, speed in her hooves
She runs, dashing through fields of pink
The sun lighting her way
Beside her is I, her lover
Keeping pace
But getting lost in her movements
The rhythm of her hips
Create a crackle with each step
I am mesmerised by her fearlessness
Time slows as I admire each gallop

My desire drives me to cause conversation
And my lover speaks to me
We speed into a tunnel
Filled with her history
Fears, wants, unspoken desires
I take it all in

Who can lasso a storm
A woman whose essence changes the wind
Whose mind is like a maze
Always connected, never ending

As we break into an open field
Other stallions, foolish as they were
Try to approach the storm
But with one motion of her legs they scramble
Their manes mangled in confusion
They cannot comprehend what it takes to lasso a storm

My lover cannot be contained
She must be approached with the wisdom of patience, and kindness
Because to lasso a storm
You must love the rain
