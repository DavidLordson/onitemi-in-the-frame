To Love Is To Understand

I love you Onitemi
In ways that cannot be achieved physically
That I would meld into your soul
To know who you are to the very core
Would be my defining achievement in marriage

To love is to understand
And I wish to understand you more
To know the words that are on your tongue before you speak them
To know when you need me
just by a glance in your direction

Oh how I seek to understand your body
To know every curve and dip, the spots that make you sing my middle name
And the spots that makes you relax after a rough day at work

They say to love is to be seen
But what is seeing without comprehension
And comprehension without understanding
That I would understand you, to the point where you are indistinguishable from myself.
