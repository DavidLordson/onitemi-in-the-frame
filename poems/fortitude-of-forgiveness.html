Fortitude Of Forgiveness

As we grow older, this is the one virtue
I pray I never lose
I see men and women each day
Hold on to resentment
Like a relay baton
Like it guarantees them
some trophy of validation

Yet I cannot bear
the thought of being mad at you for long
Never long as to exceed a few hours
For I would rather we told each other
A million apologies, than to whisper a word of unforgiveness
